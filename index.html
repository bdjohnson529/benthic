Services

Electronic Installation

Custom Electronics

Custom car alarms? No problem. 1TB hard drive to store and play music on the go? Easy. If you have an idea for a customized electronics application for your car, we will make it happen.

We repair window and wiper motors, sensors, lighting (interior and exterior), wire harnesses, ignition circuits, fuses and any other automotive electrical systems.
